The Problem

Traditional VPNs authenticate devices or shared credentials — not people. This creates blind spots, weak offboarding, and painful audits.

The Zylock Solution

Zylock binds identity, device, and session into a single control plane. Every connection is attributable, revocable, and logged.

What Zylock Does

Zylock controls who can access your private network using SSO, mTLS, and WireGuard. Your existing systems continue to control application permissions.
